*dbt-jump2def.txt*	Jump to definitions in dbt projects.

This Vim/Neovim plugin allows you to jump to definitions in dbt projects.

==============================================================================
COMMAND                                                 *dbt-jump2def-command*

DbtJump2ModelDef
	:DbtJump2ModelDef

  Jump to the model's definition when you execute it on the line with the model
  name.
